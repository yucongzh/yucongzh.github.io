The Mel-Frequency Cepstral Co-efficient (MFCC)

Steps to convert audio in MFCC:
1. Get audio in a time domain format.
2. Pre-emphasis.
3. Audio framing, assuming the audio is a stationary process for a short period of time.
5. Perform hamming window to make the audio continuous.
6. Perform FFT to convert amplitude into frequency.
7. Using mel-filterbanks to convert the frequency into mel scale.
$$\operatorname{Mel}(f)=2585 \times \log \left(1+\frac{f}{700}\right)$$
8. Perform logarithm of all filterbank energies.
9. Perform IDCT of the log filterbank energies.
10. Keep top several IDCT co-efficients, discarding the rest.
