The Kolmogorov-Smirnov Distance

The KS distance is only for probability measures on $\mathbb{R}$. $$\rho(\boldsymbol{X}, \boldsymbol{Y}):=\sup _{\boldsymbol{x} \in \mathbb{R}}\left|F_{X}(\boldsymbol{x})-F_{Y}(\boldsymbol{x})\right|$$ where $F_X(x)$ is the distribution function of X and $F_Y(x)$ is the distribution function of Y.
Note that the KS distance is never bigger than the Total Variational distance.
