The Total Variation (TV) Distance

The TV distance between two probability measures $\mu$ and $v$ on $\mathbb{R}$ is defined as: $$\operatorname{TV}(\mu, \nu):=\sup _{A \in \mathcal{B}}|\mu(A)-\nu(A)|$$ where $\mathcal{B}$ denotes the class of Borel sets.
"Largest possible difference between the probabilities that the two probability distributions can assign to the same event." —— Wikipedia
